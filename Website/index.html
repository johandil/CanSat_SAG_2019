Wildfire Detection

The summer of 2018 involved wildfires on multiple continents and California enduring the biggest wildfire ever recorded.

With global temperature on the rise, with longer droughts and with human health and property at stake, it is becoming ever more important to detect wildfires early and contain them before they run out of control.

This is our mission. The detection part at least.
We seek to build a CanSat that, amongst others, uses visual images, GPS, and a smoke detector to establish the presence and location of any wildfires.
